Fundamental Concepts

Plasma Parameters

Debye length, plasma frequency, and the fundamental scales that govern plasma behavior from laboratory to astrophysical systems.

$$\lambda_D = \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon_0 k_B T_e}{n_e e^2}}$$

Magnetohydrodynamics

The fluid description of plasma dynamics, combining the Navier-Stokes equations with Maxwell's equations for conducting fluids.

$$\frac{\partial \mathbf{B}}{\partial t} = \nabla \times (\mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{B}) + \eta \nabla^2 \mathbf{B}$$

Fusion Reactions

Nuclear fusion cross-sections, reaction rates, and the physics of controlled fusion for energy generation.

$$\langle\sigma v\rangle = \sqrt{\frac{8k_B T}{\pi \mu}} \int_0^\infty \sigma(E) E e^{-E/k_B T} dE$$
